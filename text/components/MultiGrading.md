## ![](../../images/icons/MultiGrading.png) MultiGrading

![](../../images/components/MultiGrading.png)

MultiGrading Create a grading for multiple segmentGradings. -

#### Inputs
* ##### segmentGradings [Required]
A list of segmentGradings.

#### Outputs
* ##### grading
A butterfly Grading. Connect the output to gradXYZ component to set the grading of blockMesh in X, Y or Z direction.

## ![](../../images/icons/Inlet_Boundary.png) Inlet Boundary

![](../../images/components/Inlet_Boundary.png)

Create an inlet boundary with uniform velocity value. -

* ##### velocityVec [Required]

Temperature in degrees celsius.

## ![](../../images/icons/Wind_Vector.png) Wind Vector

![](../../images/components/Wind_Vector.png)

Wind vector. -

#### Inputs
* ##### windSpeed [Required]
Wind speed in m/s at a the reference height (_refWindHeight_).
* ##### windDirection [Default]
Wind direction as Vector3D (default: 0, 1, 0).

#### Outputs
* ##### windVector
Wind Vector.

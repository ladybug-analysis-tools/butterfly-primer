## ![](../../images/icons/LocationRefinementMode.png) LocationRefinementMode

![](../../images/components/LocationRefinementMode.png)

Inside/Ouside region refinement. -

#### Inputs
* ##### mode [Default]
Refinement mode (0: inside, 1: outside).
* ##### level [Required]
Refinement level as an integer. All cells inside the surface get refined up to the level. The surface needs to be closed for this to be possible.

#### Outputs
* ##### locationRefMode
Refinement mode.
